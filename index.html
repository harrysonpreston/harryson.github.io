What is 'Micro' SaaS?

Micro-saas is quite simply a smaller, more niche version of a SaaS business. Usually a solo endeavor run by one person or a very small team. The goal is to find the smallest business problem that you can solve better, cheaper, or prettier (sleeker user interface) than the competition.

Or perhaps there is no competition because the problem is too small, with not enough ROI (return on investment) for bigger companies to tackle. There are many different types of Micro Saas companies which we'll cover in the next section.
